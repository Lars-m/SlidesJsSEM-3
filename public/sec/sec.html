Our Initial Plan

  • We plan to cover all threats in OWASP Top 10
  • We plan to do a few Hacking Light Contents (you get to play hackers ;-)
  • Securing Remote Databases (Summing up on 2 + 3 semester)
  • SSL and certificates "under the hood". Understand what you actually did when you set up your 3rd-semester site to handle SSL.
  • Understand general attack strategies such as "man in the middle" and others
  • Understand, choose and use different login strategies (Basic Authentication, JSON WEB Tokens, Oath, Login via Facebook / Google/Git etc)
  • How to handle (and not handle) Passwords
  • Understand (security) consequences of choosing a deployment/maintenance-strategy
  • Choose and use alternative platforms compared to "Infra Structure as a Service", which we get from DO
  • Rules for data protection compliance
  • Organizational aspects and responsibilities
  • Payments and "all the other things"
