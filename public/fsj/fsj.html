Form

The semester will be divided up into six periods (of varying length), each focusing on a specific area of the syllabus.

At the start of each period, you will be given a description with the learning goals (stated as questions) and a mini project or a number of smaller exercises that has to be implemented before the end of the period.

At least one period will be marked as replaceable. This will give you a chance to:

Dig into areas not planned for the semester, but with topics of your interest

What is required from you?

You are attending a full-time study, so we expect you to spend 5-8 hours back home each week (for each elective course), reading, watching videos and completing exercises :-)

Exam

The six period descriptions and your solutions form the basis for the examination. This will be supplemented with two additional questions that bridges topics from all of the periods. Together this will form 10 topics which can be "drawn" at the exam. The exam is an oral examination, with no preparation, graded using the 12 scale.

Admission requisite

  • All six period hand-ins must be handed-in an approved (min. 5 studypoints)
  • A student must obtain at least 80 StudyPoints in the course
